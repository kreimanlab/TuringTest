Instructions

In each trial of this task you will be given two words in English. One of the word is a 'cue word' and the other word is its association. The association has been made by either a human or an AI model.

Your task is to decide whether the association has been made by a human OR by AI.

You will be presented with 90 such pairs of cue word and association.

For the first 40 trials, the correct answer is provided after you submit your answer; for the rest 50 trials, no feedback is given
