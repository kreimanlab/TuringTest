Instructions

You will also need to select the topic of the conversation from five options.

Half of the speakers in this experiment are human, and half are AI chatbots (50/50 split).

BONUS OPPORTUNITY: While topic identification is required for acceptance, your BONUS will be based on your accuracy in identifying humans vs. AI chatbots. For each correctly identified trial, you will get an extra 15 cents, up to 4.95$ bonus!
